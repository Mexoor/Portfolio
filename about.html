Johans Top Three Movies of All Time!

Demolition Man

Classic '90 action movie that shows a police officer waking up from cryosleep in the year 2032 to capture a dangoruos criminal. It got not just cool action but also give and interesting world view of the future.

Stargate

An ancient ring is discoverd in Egypt with unknown origin, but it is revealed to be a gate connecting to another planet. What will await our heroes on the other side? One of my first sci-fi movies along with Star Wars

V For Vendetta

A vigilante rises up to fight for freedom and for his own personal revenge. Based on a comic book before it was cool it doesen't have as much action as you might expect but has very good worldbuilding and characters
